Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking a lesson.

Do I need to know English already?

No. I teach beginners and students who already know some English.

Are lessons online?

Yes, lessons can be online. Physical lessons may also be possible depending on location.

Do you explain in Polish?

Yes. I use Polish support to make difficult English ideas easier to understand.

How long is one lesson?

Lessons can be 60 minutes or longer depending on the student's needs.

Do you teach speaking?

Yes. Speaking confidence is one of the main goals of the lessons.
